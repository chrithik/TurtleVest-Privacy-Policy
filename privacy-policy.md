### Privacy Policy for TurtleVest 
**Effective Date: January 26, 2026**

TurtleVest is an educational mobile application created by a team of high school students to help young adults, beginners, and anyone new to money matters ("financial dummies") learn about financial literacy, investing basics, and smart money management.

This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding any information when you use our mobile app. **Please read this policy carefully.**

#### 1. Information We Collect
**We do not collect any personal information from you.**  

This includes, but is not limited to:
- No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other identifying information
- No account creation or login required
- No location data
- No device identifiers for tracking or advertising purposes
- No photos, contacts, microphone, camera, financial details, or other sensitive data

The app operates entirely offline. All educational content, simulations, quizzes, and tools are stored and processed locally on your device—no data is sent to us or any servers.

#### 2. Information We Do Not Collect
To be explicit, we **do not** collect, store, or transmit:
- Any personal data
- Any financial account information, investment details, or transaction history
- Any browsing history, search queries, or usage data linked to you personally
- Any data for advertising, marketing, or profiling purposes

#### 3. Third-Party Services
We do **not** use any third-party services that collect personal data.  

If the app includes basic crash reporting or aggregated usage insights through Apple's built-in systems (e.g., App Analytics), such data is completely anonymous and handled solely by Apple in accordance with its privacy policy:  
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

We do **not** integrate analytics tools (such as Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel), advertising SDKs, or any other services that track users.

#### 4. Age Appropriateness
This app is intended for users aged **13 and older**. It contains educational content about financial topics that may include discussions of risk, markets, and investing concepts suitable for high school students and young adults. The app is **not** directed at children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect or process any information from users under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the app.

#### 5. Security
Since we collect, store, or transmit **no personal data**, there is no risk of us exposing your information through data breaches or unauthorized access on our side.  

All app content and user interactions (such as quiz progress or saved notes, if any) remain exclusively on your device. We follow basic secure development practices, including:
- Avoiding unnecessary permissions
- Not implementing server-side features or data syncing
- Regularly testing the app for stability and basic vulnerabilities during development

We cannot control the security of your device itself (e.g., if it is lost, stolen, or compromised by malware). We recommend keeping your device's operating system updated and using standard device security features (passcode, biometric lock, etc.).

#### 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. The updated version will be posted within the app and on this page with a revised effective date. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
