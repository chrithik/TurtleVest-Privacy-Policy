## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. Is this app using real money?
**No.** Turtlevest is purely an educational tool. All market simulations, portfolio strategies, and "wealth-building challenges" use virtual currency. The app does not involve real-world trading, investing, or financial transactions.

### 2. Who is Turtlevest for?
Turtlevest is designed by young investors, for young investors. It is ideal for high school and college students looking for a clear, engaging, and bite-sized way to learn financial literacy.

### 3. How do I restart a game or simulation?
You can find a **Restart** button within most interactive modules (like the Wealth Builder Challenge). Additionally, navigating back to the Home screen will reset your local session state for most games.

### 4. Why should I use Turtlevest on an iPad?
While Turtlevest is compatible with iPhones, the **iPad** experience offers a larger layout that is optimized for reading our detailed financial courses and viewing full-scale portfolio charts.

## Technical Support & Bug Reporting
If the app is crashing or a feature isn't working as expected:
1. Ensure you are using the latest version of the app from the App Store.
2. Try closing and restarting the app.
3. If the issue remains, please reach out to us using the contact email provided at the top of this page.

## Investment Disclaimer
Turtlevest is an educational platform designed to build foundational financial knowledge. **It does not provide professional financial or investment advice.** The portfolios, historical returns, and simulations presented are for educational exploration only and do not involve actual money or real-world trading. Rules and market behaviors may differ significantly in different countries; please consult with a qualified financial professional for personalized advice in your specific region.
